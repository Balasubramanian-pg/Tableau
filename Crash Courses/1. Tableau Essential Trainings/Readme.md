Curt Frye dives into the power of Tableau with this in-depth course crafted to elevate your data visualization capabilities. Explore ways to connect, clean, and prepare data from various sources. Learn how to construct compelling visualizations, from basic plots to advanced maps and dashboards, tailored for impactful presentation and decision-making. Discover how to implement calculated fields, utilize parameters, and summarize your data efficiently with highlights, trendlines, and reference lines. Deepen your understanding of how to customize charts, maps, and tables to bring clarity and visibility to complex data. Benefit from hands-on examples and storytelling techniques, and learn to leverage Tableau's full potential for influencing business outcomes. When you complete this course, you will be able to create informative and attractive Tableau dashboards that promote greater engagement from stakeholders.
Learning objectives
Define data connections and create visualizations.
Create custom calculations and analyze data.
Sort and filter data.
Define groups, sets, and geographic layouts.
Summarize data on dashboards.
